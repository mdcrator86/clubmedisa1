💡 Board Highlights — 3-Year Performance

  • Occupancy on strong upward trend: 27.3% → 35.9% → 42.3% year-on-year. Villa demand is consistently growing.
  • Revenue dipped –4.8% in FY26 despite record occupancy: Higher bookings (132 vs 113 nights) but avg nightly rate fell from ₹32,210 to ₹26,258. Pricing review is strongly recommended.
  • December is peak month every year: Dec 2025 generated ₹8.82 L at 71% occupancy — all-time high single-month performance.
  • Total owner earnings over 3 years: ₹72.19 L with ₹6.58 L recoverable as TDS credit in ITR.
  • Net villa cash flow (May 2024–Mar 2026): ₹26.35 L after RFTPM payments, staff salaries, and BMW EMI.
