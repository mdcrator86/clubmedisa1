📊 Key Observations — 3-Year Commission Flow

  • FY 2024-25 was the peak year: ₹36.40 L gross sales and ₹29.24 L net to owner — a 89.8% jump over FY 2023-24 (partial year).
  • FY 2025-26 held strong: Slight dip to ₹34.66 L gross (–4.8%) but owner still received ₹27.19 L — a healthy consolidated year.
  • GST is additive: ₹9.59 L collected over FY25 & FY26 flows through to the owner — it does not reduce Amount Due.
  • TDS of ₹6.58 L is fully claimable in ITR under Section 194-I — real cash recoverable via tax returns.
  • December is the banker: Dec 2023 (₹5.56 L), Dec 2024 (₹5.54 L), Dec 2025 (₹7.56 L) — highest month every year.

  • December 2025 broke all records: ₹8.82 L gross → ₹7.56 L due to owner — highest single-month payout in villa history.
